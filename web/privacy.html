StripCall Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

StripCall is a fencing tournament assistance app that helps crew chiefs and armorers manage strip problems during events. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the app:

  • Name — Your first and last name, used to identify who created problems and sent messages within your crew.
  • Email address — Used for account creation, login, and password recovery. We do not send marketing emails.
  • Phone number — Used solely to send SMS notifications when you expressly request them. We never send unsolicited messages.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to provide the StripCall service:

  • Authenticate your account and manage login sessions
  • Display your name to other crew members when you create problems or send messages
  • Send SMS notifications to your phone number when you opt in

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate StripCall. These providers process your data only as needed to deliver their services to us:

  • Supabase — Database hosting and user authentication
  • Twilio — SMS message delivery (phone numbers of users who request SMS notifications)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — Push notification delivery

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase's cloud infrastructure with row-level security policies that restrict access to authorized users. All data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as you maintain an active account. Event and problem data is retained for reporting purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Opt out of SMS notifications at any time within the app

Children's Privacy

StripCall is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through the app or by updating the date at the top of this page.
